\\
Just across the road from the main area lay a few boulders on the banks of the River. Beware that at high flow rates most of the area will be underwater. Since the River is dam controlled the water level can shift rapidly. Consult the USGS flow charts for below green peter damn to know when the river will be low.\\\\
\\\\
See driving directions for the Garden Main area.\\
